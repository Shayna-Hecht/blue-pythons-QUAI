About QUAI

Introduction

QUAI is a combination of 'QU' standing for Question, and 'AI' standing for Artifcial Intelligence. It is read as 'Quae,' a word in latin meaning 'What,' which leads to what our project is all about.

Main Function

QUAI inputs a question and rephrases it for the user. For example, if 'What is the biggest country in the world?' is inputted, it might output 'Which nation is the largest nation in the world?'

Use Cases & Expansions

QUAI is mostly used for academic purposes including studying, testing. For example, students may prepare for the exam by solving rephrased questions to understand the material better and test their knowledge.

About the Dataset

The dataset was provided by Quora through a website called Kaggle. We collected the questions that had a similar meaning, and trained our model until it was able to generate a rephrased question on its own. The dataset contained 149263 lines of questions after processing.
